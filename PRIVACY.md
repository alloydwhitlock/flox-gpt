# Privacy Policy for FloxGPT

**Last Updated:** January 9, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the FloxGPT custom GPT ("the GPT") handles information when you use it to search the FloxHub package catalog.

## What This GPT Does

FloxGPT is a ChatGPT custom GPT that helps users search for packages in the [FloxHub](https://hub.flox.dev) package catalog. It provides an interface to query publicly available package information.

## Information Collection

### What We Collect

**Search Queries**: When you ask the GPT to search for packages, your search terms are sent to the FloxHub API (`hub.flox.dev`) to retrieve results.

### What We Don't Collect

- No personal information is collected by this GPT
- No user accounts or authentication required
- No cookies or tracking mechanisms
- No analytics or usage tracking by the GPT author

## Third-Party Services

This GPT makes API requests to:

- **FloxHub** (`hub.flox.dev`) - Operated by [Flox](https://flox.dev)
  - Your search queries are sent to FloxHub's public API
  - FloxHub's own privacy practices apply to data they receive
  - See [Flox's Privacy Policy](https://flox.dev/privacy) for details

- **OpenAI/ChatGPT** - The GPT runs on OpenAI's platform
  - Your conversations are subject to [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy)
  - OpenAI may retain conversation data per their policies

## Data Retention

This GPT does not store any data. Search queries are:
- Processed in real-time
- Sent to FloxHub to retrieve results
- Not logged or retained by the GPT author

## Data Security

- All API requests to FloxHub use HTTPS encryption
- No sensitive data is requested or transmitted
- The GPT only accesses publicly available package information

## Children's Privacy

This GPT is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted to this document with an updated "Last Updated" date.
